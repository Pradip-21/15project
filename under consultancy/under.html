New Era International Education Consultancy Pvt. Ltd.
Aug 1, 2024
Welcome to New Era Education, your trusted partner in navigating the exciting world of international education. Established in 2019, we are a leading educational consultancy dedicated to empowering students like you to pursue their academic dreams abroad. With our main office located in Putalisadak, Kathmandu, and branches in Pulchowk, Chitwan, and Kohalpur, Banke, we have established ourselves as a reliable and reputable resource for students across Nepal.
Our Mission
At New Era Education, our mission is simple yet ambitious: to guide and support students in achieving their academic goals and unlocking their full potential. We believe that every student deserves access to quality education and the opportunity to pursue their passions and aspirations on a global scale. Through personalized guidance, expert counseling, and comprehensive support services, we aim to make the dream of studying abroad a reality for students from all walks of life.
Our Values
Our values serve as the foundation of everything we do at New Era Education. Integrity, excellence, innovation, and student-centeredness are at the core of our operations, guiding our interactions with students and shaping our commitment to providing exceptional service. We believe in upholding the highest ethical standards, delivering excellence in every aspect of our work, fostering a culture of innovation to meet evolving needs, and always putting the needs of our students first.
