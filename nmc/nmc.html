In order to meet the country's need for a superior educational institution that could provide for worthy students, Liverpool International College was founded in 2010. The idea of a math laboratory was first introduced by Liverpool International College. The most recent records for each student are kept by LEN in a separate information center.


Only the parents of the individual children have access to this information on the web, which is provided with high security choices. The college has also modified personal smart card systems so that students can access the library and track their attendance. The faculty of Liverpool International Education is made up of highly skilled and knowledgeable individuals with backgrounds in academic achievement from prestigious colleges and universities.
